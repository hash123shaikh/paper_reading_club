# Paper Reading Club

Personal repository for systematic reading and analysis of research papers in AI and cancer research, with focus on medical imaging, radiomics, and radiation oncology.

## Structure

Each paper folder contains:
- Original paper (PDF)
- Presentation (PPT) - Key findings and methodology
- White paper (Document) - Detailed analysis, learnings, and future directions

## Topics of Interest

- AI in cancer imaging
- Radiomics and feature extraction
- Radiation therapy planning
- Medical image segmentation
- Machine learning in oncology

## Purpose

To systematically read, understand, and document research papers while building structured knowledge that can be referenced for future research and clinical applications.
